ABOUT US

Building digital experiences
with passion & precision

We are João and Gonçalo, two UX/UI Design students passionate about creating intuitive, user-centered, and visually engaging digital experiences. Our goal is to transform ideas into functional and meaningful interfaces that balance aesthetics, usability, and accessibility.

Beyond design, we continuously explore emerging trends, design methodologies, and innovative approaches to improve our craft. We are committed to creating digital solutions that not only look appealing but also provide seamless and effective user experiences. We believe that great design is built around people, combining creativity, research, and problem-solving to deliver products that make a real impact.

OUR JOURNEY

Career Timeline

2019

Started the Journey

We began our journey at Celfocus as junior UX/UI designers, where we were introduced to the fundamentals of digital product design. During this experience, we explored core principles of user experience and interface design, while developing a solid understanding of modern design tools and workflows.

2020

Siemens

At Siemens, we focused on strengthening our UX/UI design skills within large-scale, enterprise environments. We worked on designing and improving responsive digital interfaces, ensuring consistency, usability, and accessibility across complex systems.

2022

Nike App

We began a design project inspired by the Nike app, focusing on creating a modern and engaging mobile experience. The goal was to explore how strong branding, smooth navigation, and user-centered design can come together to enhance the overall user journey.

2024-NOW

Mercedes

We are currently working on a design project inspired by Mercedes, focusing on crafting a premium digital experience that reflects innovation, precision, and elegance. The goal is to translate the brand’s identity into a refined and intuitive user interface.
